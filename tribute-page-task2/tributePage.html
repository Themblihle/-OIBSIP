Nyari Samushonga

CEO of WeThinkCode_

Nyari Samushonga is the CEO of WeThinkCode, a South African tech
academy that operates in Joburg, Durban and Cape Town.
As an Entrepreneur and Tech Executive, Nyari is passionate about
seeing Africa take its place as a maker of cutting edge technology.
She has accelerated the recruitment of women students to the point of
parity within the academy.

In her role as CEO of WeThinkCode_ she has driven effective
initiatives to scale the impact of the organisation.
These include doubling the recruitment capacity and launching a home
grown curriculum that caters to the South African market.
